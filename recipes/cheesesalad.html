Salade Avec Fromage Froid!

A must have at your next family gathering!

It's creamy and crunchy in all the wrong ways.

BONUS! It's also super stinky.

Ingredients

  • 2 2/3 cups cottage cheese
  • 8 ounces blue cheese
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons chives
  • 2 teaspoons grated lemon peel
  • 1 teaspoon barbecue spice
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cups raw broccoli florets
  • Green pepper strips, to garnish

Steps

  1. Let cheeses stand at room temperature for 30 min-utes.
  2. Place in blender container with buttermilk, chives, lemon peel, barbecue spice, and Worcester-shire.
  3. Process at medium speed until mixture is smooth.
  4. Transfer to freezer tray. Freeze at least 3 hours.
  5. Remove from freezer 15 minutes before serv-ing. Unmold on serving platter. Surround with broc-coli florets. Garnish with green pepper strips. Divide evenly.

Makes 8 luncheon servings.
