Puréed Nut Spread and Grape Relish Reduction Paired With a Brioche Bun

A Modern Delicacy

An absolute must for any master chef, this recipe has been carefully prepared to ensure the finished product is nothing less than superb!

Ingredients

  • Brioche Bun (see below)
  • Puréed Nut Spread (recipe)
  • Grape Relish Reduction (recipe)

Our very own recipe for brioche buns can be found here.

Steps

  1. Cut the brioche bun in half.
  2. Carefully spread the pureé on one half of the bun.
  3. Do the same with the reduction on the other half of the bun.
  4. Carefully join the two halves together so that the bun is whole once again.
  5. Bon appetit!
