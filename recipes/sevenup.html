Seven-Up & Milk!

a little TRICK that makes a TREAT

Mothers know that this is a wholesome combination. The addition of 7-Up gives milk a new flavour appeal that especially pleases children.

The 7-Up adds a light and delicate flavour making a delicious blended food drink.

Ingredients

  • Whole Milk
  • 7-Up

Steps

  1. Ensure both the milk and 7-Up are chilled.
  2. Fill half of a tall glass with milk.
  3. Pour the 7-Up gently into the milk filling the remainder of the glass. Note: equal parts of milk and 7-Up should be used.
  4. Enjoy!
